Life Moves Fast. Capture It.

With the constant stream of information, notifications, and communication, we find ourselves caught in a whirlwind of multitasking and rapid decision-making. Despite more and more advanced ways to document and record our surroundings, this breakneck speed can make it challenging to find moments of reflection and genuine connection.

This project focuses on the power of film photography to encourage us to slow down and appreciate the little glimmers of goodness in our everyday.
