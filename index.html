Life Moves Fast

Our digital age has dramatically transformed the process of taking and sharing photographs. It is easier than ever to snap shots at any moment, but this convenience can inadvertently detach us from the memories themselves. In an era bombarded by social media filled with staged and filtered content, we are losing a sense of authenticity and genuine connection with photos. And instead of flipping through albums filled with memories, we are often overwhelmed by cluttered phone camera reels and fill our 'Memories' with moments we don't hold the same connection to, or intention behind. This shift can have significant implications for the way our generation experiences and cherishes memories.

Put it in Focus
with Film

ISO Film Co. is dedicated to reviving the timeless art of film photography in the modern world.

More than a photography brand, ISO Film Co. represents an ethos, rooted in the art of noticing and appreciating the unfiltered, genuine moments in our everyday lives.

Designs

With a visual identity that is one part nostalgic, one part contemporary, these products are designed to invite young millennials and photography enthusiasts to explore the world of film photography.

Products include quality film and camera accessories to themed kits accompanied by zine photo guides that not only provide photography tips but also lessons in the art of noticing and capturing those precious, often overlooked moments.
