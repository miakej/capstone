Celebrating life's unfiltered moments with film

Life Moves Fast


Our digital age has profoundly transformed the art of taking and sharing photographs, reshaping our relationship with the moments we encounter in our daily lives.

Modern digital cameras have made it easier than ever to snap high quality shots at any moment, but this convenience can inadvertently detach us from the memories themselves. In an era bombarded by social media filled with staged and filtered moments, we have lost a sense of authenticity and genuine connection with photos. And instead of flipping through albums filled with candid memories, we are often overwhelmed by cluttered phone camera reels, and rely on algorithms to sort through and notify us of our own memories. This shift can have significant implications for the way our generation experiences and cherishes memories.

Slow Down with Film

Iso Film Co. is more than a photography brand: it is also an ethos, rooted in the art of noticing and appreciating the unfiltered, genuine moments in our everyday lives.

With a visual identity that is one part nostalgic, one part contemporary, Iso Film Co. is dedicated to reviving the timeless art of film photography in the modern world. The range of products are designed to empower young millennials and photography enthusiasts to explore the world of film photography: from quality film and camera accessories to themed kits accompanied by zine photo guides that not only provide photography tips but also lessons in the art of noticing and capturing those precious, often overlooked moments.
